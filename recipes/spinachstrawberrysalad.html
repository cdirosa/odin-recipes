Spinach & Strawberry Salad

Description

My family loves this spinach strawberry salad all year-round, if we can find strawberries. Even the grandchildren love this salad. Quick and easy.

Ingredients

  • 2 bunches spinach, rinsed and torn into bite-size pieces
  • 4 cups sliced strawberries
  • ½ cup vegetable oil
  • ½ cup white sugar
  • ¼ cup white wine vinegar
  • 2 tablespoons sesame seeds
  • 1 tablespoon poppy seeds
  • ¼ teaspoon paprika

Steps

  1. Cut the strawberries in half
  2. Toss together spinach and strawberries in a large bowl.
  3. Whisk oil, sugar, vinegar, sesame seeds, poppy seeds, and paprika together in a medium bowl.
  4. Pour over the spinach and strawberries, and toss to coat.
